The New World

Welcome to The New World, a fantastical realm where the seven elements intertwine and shape the world.
In ancient times, mortals were bestowed with extraordinary elemental powers by the Archons.
These abilities allowed them to transform the wild lands into a thriving homeland.
Nevertheless, the world underwent a dramatic upheaval 500 years ago, leading to the collapse of an ancient civilization.
While the calamity that befell the world has subsided, true peace remains elusive, and the quest for restoration continues.

Sky City

Soar above the clouds and explore a breathtaking metropolis in
the sky. Sky City is a haven for those who dream of a life above the
ordinary. With its sleek skyscrapers, lush parks, and stunning views,
it's no wonder this city is a popular destination for tourists and
adventurers alike.

Liyue

An abundant port nestled in the eastern reaches of the New World,
Liyue boasts a captivating landscape defined by majestic mountains, a
stone forest, expansive plains, and meandering rivers. This rich tapestry
of natural wonders unfolds its unique charm across all four
seasons. Each passing season graces Liyue with its distinct beauty,
creating a picturesque panorama.

Jolville

Welcome to the majestic Joltville, a verdant haven nestled atop
a sky-high peak. This awe-inspiring image captures the essence of this fantastical realm,
where nature and architecture intertwine in a breathtaking tapestry.
The centerpiece of the image is undoubtedly the Joltville castle, a magnificent
stone fortress that seems to sprout organically from the mountain's crest.
Its weathered ramparts and towering spires whisper of ancient legends and bygone battles.
Lush greenery carpets the castle's base, cradling it in a
vibrant embrace.
