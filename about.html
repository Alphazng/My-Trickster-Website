Game Features

Epic Fantasy Adventure

Throughout your odyssey, engage with intriguing characters, confront formidable adversaries, and unveil the hidden history of The New World. Each step of your journey will bring you closer to understanding the enigmatic forces at play, as well as your pivotal role in the unfolding narrative. Immerse yourself in the captivating landscapes, uncover forgotten civilizations, and forge alliances as you strive to untangle the intricate web of mysteries that define your solitary adventure.

World to Discover

Roam freely through the expansive landscapes, where the ability to soar through the open skies, dive into pristine waters, and scale towering peaks adds a dynamic layer to your exploration. Venture beyond the well-trodden routes to unveil the concealed wonders of a world teeming with awe-inspiring marvels and tantalizing mysteries waiting to be unearthed. Whether it's gliding over vast canyons, navigating underwater realms, or conquering challenging summits, your journey promises an unparalleled adventure filled with surprises at every turn.

Travel Alone or Together

Engage in thrilling combat scenarios, taking on formidable adversaries either as a lone warrior or by rallying friends to confront perilous monsters together. With seamless 4-player cross-play compatibility across iOS, Android, and PC, the choice is yours on how to assemble your team and strategize for epic battles, ensuring a dynamic and cooperative gaming experience across multiple platforms.
